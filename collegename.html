Greenfield University

Mumbai, Maharashtra

About the College

Greenfield University is one of the top institutions in India, offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Established in 2005, the university is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and excellent placement records.

Courses Offered

  • BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)
  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
  • BSc-IT (Bachelor of Science in Information Technology)
  • BCom (Bachelor of Commerce)
  • BA (Bachelor of Arts)
  • BSc (Bachelor of Science)

Admission Process

Admissions are based on merit and entrance exam scores. For undergraduate programs, JEE Main and KCET scores are considered. For postgraduate programs, CAT/MAT scores are required.
